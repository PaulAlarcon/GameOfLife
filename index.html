Welcome to the Game Of Life


Ever since I found out about Conway's Game Of Life, I was facinated by all the different conbinations it can produce, so I decided to create my own implementation. Enjoy


The Rules

1. Underpopulation

A living cell with less than 2 living neighbours dies.

2. Survives

A living cell with 3 living neighbours survives.

3. Overpopulation

A living cell with more than 3 living neighbours dies.

4. Reproduction

A dead cell with three living neighbours becomes alive
